Diet Plans

Mediterranean Diet

The Mediterranean diet consists of mainly eating fruits and veggies, alongside fish seafood as the main source of nutrition. This diet has been known to provide anti-inflammatory benefits as this diet focuses on a reduced consumption of processed foods.

Carnivore Diet

The carnivore diet is a highly restrictive eating plan. This plan consists of eating animal-based foods, it eliminates all plant-based foods. This diet has been popular as some believe it leads to weight loss and muscle gain.

Vegan Diet

The vegan diet is a plant-based eating diet where you don't eat any animal products, this includes dairy. Inidviduals eat nuts, lentils and other legumes mainly to meet their protein needs. A benefit of this diet is weight loss.
