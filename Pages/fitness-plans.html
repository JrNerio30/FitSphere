Strength Training

Strength training involves a combination of exercises that use weights like dumbbells, barbells, kettlebells, and other resistance tools to build muscle and increase strength. By progressively lifting heavier weights, individuals can increase muscle mass, increase strength, and achieve better physical fitness.

Pilates

Pilates is a low-impact exercise that usually requires minimal equipment, such as mats, resistance bands, or reformer machines. The primary focus of of these workouts is to build core strength, which helps improve flexibility, posture, and mobility. Pilates is an good choice for individuals with mobility issues or those looking for a gentle workout as it does not require heavy lifting.

Calisthenics

Calisthenics focuses on building full-body strength and endurance using only your body weight. This can be achieved by performing various movements such as push-ups and squats. As an individual advances, they can progress to more advanced compound movements that require significant stability and control.
